About Me

Hi! My name is Andrey and I'm an artist, designer, and 3D-visualiser currently based in Moscow. For the past four years, I had been a full-time environmental design student at the Moscow State Stroganov Academy of Design and Applied Arts. During this time, I enjoyed working on both coursework and commissioned external projects, some of which you will have seen in this portfolio.

Now, having recently graduated, I'm taking on new projects, both personal and alongside clients, hoping to grow and expand in the field. In my free time, you can find me playing any string instrument.
