ABOUT US

At caregiVR, we focus on creating useful technologies and tools for family members/caregivers tending to loved ones suffering from dementia.

Our first product uses Virtual Reality (VR) to create immersive experiences that help soothe anxiety for Persons with Dementia (PwD) and uses methods which slows the disease progression.

Through VR, we merge Reminisence Therapy (RT) with exercise and social collaboration - some of the only clinical proven means of slowing the onset of the disease - to create a cognitive training program for those suffering from this debilitating disease.
